ABOUT US

“Great products
Great service”

Based in Bali since 1996 we have a vast network of manufacturers and material sources in order to facilitate your production. Hand-made arts and crafts are our specialty. Our mission is to offer the most competitive prices for any production size with no minimum quantity required, an ideal condition for the current times.

We pride ourselves in being extremely detail oriented while offering the best customer service in every step of the way. Our main activity is assisting brands across all areas of the fashion industry: Design consulting, sample production and mass manufacturing of a wide range of clothing and products. We even manage the shipping so you can receive your order delivered right at your door.

Wether you manage a hotel, a shop or any kind of company and are in need of great, cheap and quality production don't hesitate to contact us!
